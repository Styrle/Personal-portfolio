Preactiv is a application used by the NHS to help those get fit before surgery. My primary role was to work as a UX designer to push the applications visual design whilst keeping it accessible to the older audiance. The primary goal was to create an application in which users would be guided through a series of exercises every day, this being determined by an algorithm.

The project ran over 7 months working on the UX/UI process and later on, creating videos for patients and the landing pages for the website to promote the application

I worked closely with team leaders and the development team to ensure all concepts where handled appropriately, this included handing over stylesheets, designs accross multiple breakpoints, clickable prototypes and interactions
