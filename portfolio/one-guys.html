Working with the guys at One I created an online digital platform, from which they could reach new members, including the development of members only content.

The first step was to look at competitors and and analyse what was already avaliable in this market. From there I begin to build up a potential sitemap. From the initial wireframes I worked closely with the client to align the imagery with their branding. This allowed us to develop a clear user flow for the website, before moving to the hi-fi development stage.

The second step consisted of implimentation using Wordpress to bring the business to life using custom plugins and payment intergration system (Stripe) to create the perfect website for them and their members.
