ValHQ are a esports company who host tournaments online and during the pandemic their buisness has really taken off. As a result they where looking for something more stable for users who wish to compete in their tournaments.

The people at Val already had a great vision for what they wanted from the website and as a result gave me great direction in how I should create the website using templating and blog plugins.

Throughout the development of the website I worked closely with management to make sure there strict deadline was met and that the key features they wanted to implement where done so, also giving them room for scalability in future developments.
