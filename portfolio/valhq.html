ValHQ

Project type

Web Development

Year

2021

Skills

Web Development

ValHQ are an Esports company who host tournaments online, and during the pandemic their business has really taken off. As a result they were looking for a more stable website, for users who wish to compete in their tournaments, than their previous platform.

The people at Val already had a great vision for what they wanted from the website and as a result gave me clear direction as to how I should create it using templating and blog plugins.

Throughout the development of the website I worked closely with management team to ensure that strict deadline's were met, and that the key features they wanted to implement where achieved. This approach gives them room for scalability in future developments.
